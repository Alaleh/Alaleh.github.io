Alaleh Ahmadian

I am Alaleh Ahmadian, currently a Ph.D. Student at Washington State University. I am very fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Jana Doppa.

My general research interests are in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Sequential Decision-making under Uncertainty with applications to real-world problems in engineering and scientific domains. My current research focuses on developing fast and effective Bayesian Optimization (BO) methods and applying them to real-world problems to accelerate hardware design and 3D printing of biomedical structures.

Previously, I was a computer science undergraduate student at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, Iran.
